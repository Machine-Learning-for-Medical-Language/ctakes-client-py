

The patient was brought to the endoscopy suite, where they were placed in left
lateral Sims position and underwent IV sedation. Digital rectal examination
was performed, which showed no masses. The colonoscope was placed in the
rectum and advanced under direct vision to the cecum. The remainder of the
colonoscopy was normal. On withdrawal of the scope, there was no evidence of
any mass, lesions, mucosal abnormalities, friability, or polyps. The patient
was then awakened and taken from the endoscopy suite in stable condition.
