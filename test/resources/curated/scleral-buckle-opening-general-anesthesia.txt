

PROCEDURE IN DETAIL: After appropriate operative consent was obtained, the
patient was brought supine to the operating room and placed on the operating
room table. Induction of general anesthesia via endotracheal intubation was
then accomplished without difficulty. The patient 's right eye was prepped and
draped in a sterile ophthalmic fashion and the procedure begun. A wire lid
speculum was inserted into the right eye and a 360-degree conjunctival
peritomy was performed at the limbus. The 4 rectus muscles were looped and
isolated using 2-0 silk suture. The retinal periphery was then inspected via
indirect ophthalmoscopy.
